Idua Assang Community

Idua Assang is a community located in the Oron local government Area of, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. Home to the National museum Oron, Oron Beach Market, major seafood and traveling port/beach which in the past harbored ships from Oron to Calabar and different states in the country and host traders from different parts of the country. facebook

GOOD TO KNOW ABOUT THE COMMUNITY

The community has played host to major events in Nigerian history but lacked the most important change agent that every developed, developing community, state, and countries use as a driving change for a better society and advancement which is Education. Until the 2nd of August 2019, the Idua Assang community has been without a high school/ secondary school when patriotic community youths came together with the support of the community Chiefs and Officials to set up a school the IDUA COMMUNITY HIGH SCHOOL ORON, which resumed learning on the 16th of September 2019. Presently the school has about one hundred and seventy students and keeps growing. This school has maintained steady growth but there's a lack and inaccessibility to study materials for the student who are from underprivileged homes, the nearest library that is accessible by the public is at the state capital (UYO) which is about an hour drive by road and seemingly not trackable hence, the need to set up a library to aid the students and encourage parents to take education as an important tool in shaping the life of their children. google instagram

THE AIM OF A LIBRARY PROJECT

Encourage parents to send their children to this school, by this, illiteracy will be drained drastically among young people in the community.
